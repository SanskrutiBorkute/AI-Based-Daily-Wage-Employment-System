# Abstract

Finding daily wage work is still a challenge for many workers. Most of them depend on local contractors or personal contacts to get jobs, which often leads to irregular employment and unfair wages. At the same time, employers struggle to find skilled and reliable workers quickly.

The AI-Based Daily Wage Employment System, named **KaamSetu**, is designed to solve this problem by creating a digital platform where workers and employers can connect easily. The system uses Artificial Intelligence to recommend suitable jobs to workers and suggest the best workers for employers based on skills, experience, location, and availability.

The platform aims to reduce unemployment among daily wage workers, simplify the hiring process, and create a transparent and efficient employment ecosystem.

---

# 1. Introduction

Daily wage workers form a major part of the workforce in sectors such as construction, agriculture, housekeeping, plumbing, electrical work, and many others. Despite their importance, many workers face difficulties in finding regular employment opportunities.

Traditional methods of finding work are often slow, unreliable, and limited to local networks. Employers also spend significant time searching for suitable workers.

KaamSetu provides a modern solution by bringing workers and employers onto a single platform. Through AI-powered recommendations, the system helps workers find jobs faster while helping employers recruit suitable workers efficiently.

---

# 2. Literature Review

Several job portals and recruitment platforms already exist, but most of them focus on permanent or professional jobs. Daily wage workers often remain underserved by such systems.

Studies show that Artificial Intelligence can improve recruitment processes by matching job requirements with candidate skills more accurately. Location-based services and digital employment platforms have also proven effective in reducing hiring time and increasing accessibility.

These findings inspired the development of KaamSetu, which combines AI-based matching with a user-friendly employment platform specifically designed for daily wage workers.

# 3. Methodology

The system works through three main users:

### Worker

Workers can register on the platform and create their profiles by providing information such as:

* Skills
* Experience
* Location
* Availability
* Expected wages

### Employer

Employers can:

* Post job opportunities
* Search for workers
* Review worker profiles
* Hire suitable candidates

### AI Module

The AI module analyzes worker and job information to:

* Recommend suitable jobs to workers
* Suggest the best workers to employers
* Provide wage recommendations
* Improve matching accuracy

This creates a faster and more efficient hiring process for both parties.

### Working Process

1. Workers and employers register on the platform.
2. Employers post job requirements.
3. Workers apply for available jobs.
4. The AI system analyzes profiles and job requirements.
5. Suitable matches are recommended.
6. Employers select workers and complete hiring.
7. Ratings and feedback are recorded for future recommendations.

# 5. Results and Discussion

The developed system successfully provides a digital solution for daily wage employment management.

Key outcomes include:

* Faster worker-employer matching
* Easy job posting and application process
* Improved employment opportunities
* AI-based recommendations
* Better transparency in hiring
* User-friendly interface

The system demonstrates how technology can simplify workforce management and support daily wage workers in finding regular employment.

---

# 6. Limitations

Although the system performs well, it has some limitations:

* Internet access is required.
* AI recommendations depend on available user data.
* Some workers may not be familiar with digital platforms.
* Location data may not always be accurate.
